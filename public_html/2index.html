My name is Ibbo Abdoli, an Automation and Industrial Electrical Technician based in Södertälje. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Systems Engineering and have hands-on experience in industrial automation, embedded systems, and production technology.

Since January 2025, I’ve been working at Lantmännen through Elektroautomatik, where I specialize in:

  • Troubleshooting and optimization of industrial automation systems
  • Electrical inspections based on Swedish standards (SS 436 40 00)
  • Thermographic scanning and electrical risk assessments

What sets me apart is my deep curiosity, strong commitment to learning, and the ability to solve complex technical problems under pressure. I thrive in environments that require critical thinking, fast action, and attention to detail.

In every project, my goal is to deliver practical solutions, clear documentation, and real results that contribute to the success of both the team and the client.
