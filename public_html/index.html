How I Work

Observe, Isolate, Verify, Document, Prevent

A practical workflow for service work in production environments, built around fast fault isolation, clear verification and useful documentation for the next maintenance action.

01

Observe

Understand the symptom, machine state, alarms, operator input and recent history before changing anything.

02

Isolate

Separate electrical, mechanical and automation-related causes with structured checks.

03

Verify

Confirm signals, safety states, PLC/I/O status, sensors, actuators and communication paths.

04

Document

Write clear findings, actions performed and recommended next steps for maintenance follow-up.

05

Prevent

Turn repeated faults and field observations into practical improvement ideas.
